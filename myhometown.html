Kampong Cham

Geography

is a province (khaet) of Cambodia located on the central lowlands of the Mekong River.

Etymology

Kampong Cham means "Port of the Chams" in Khmer. Kampong means port, harbor. Cham refers to the ethnic Cham people living in the province.

Tourist attraction

Kampong Cham has a lot of tourist attractions that relate to history. For example Women and Men mountain also known as “Phnom pros phnom srey”, Nokor bachey temple, Kampong cham port or river bank, Koh pen, Teuk chhar, etc.


Food and Spacialty

Kampong Cham’s foods have their own uniqueness. Cricket insects and Ah ping or giant spider Tarantulas fries from Skun are pretty famous. Jeruk maman eating it with roasted fish is the best. Leas hal really good. And lastly Somlor Kako because this province has lots of vegetables and our specialty!
